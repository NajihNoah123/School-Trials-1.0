GRADUATION

The final trial is over. The school falls silent as the announcement echoes through the empty halls.

"Congratulations to our graduate. You have proven yourself worthy through intelligence, adaptability, and understanding the true purpose of these trials."

The principal appears, no longer a distorted voice but a solemn figure in a dark suit.

"These trials were never about elimination. They were about finding the one student who could see beyond competition - who understood that true success comes from cooperation, empathy, and the willingness to help others even in the face of personal risk."

You look around at the empty classroom. "What happened to the others?"

The principal smiles. "They are safe. No one was ever in real danger. The eliminations were simulated - a necessary illusion to test your character under pressure."

As you exit the school, you see all the other students waiting outside, applauding. They were part of the test all along.

The school shimmers and fades like a mirage, revealing a futuristic facility behind it.

"Welcome to the real academy," says the principal. "Now your true education begins."
