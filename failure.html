SCHOOL TRIALS

ELIMINATION

The buzzer sounds. Your time has run out. The trial is over, and you have failed.

"Student eliminated," announces the cold, mechanical voice over the intercom.

The other students look away as the floor beneath you opens up. You fall into darkness, the screams of the remaining students fading above you.

As you fall, you wonder what will happen to the others. Will any of them survive the School Trials?

The darkness envelops you completely. This is the end of your journey.
